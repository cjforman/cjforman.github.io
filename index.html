Rubiconomy

In the following app, agents build towers from cubes and the height of an agents tower represents their wealth, for which they receive a dividend.

The total wealth of the economy is the number on the right and the wealth assigned to each agent is under the tower.

Each agent can choose to do one labor and one finance move per turn.

Labor:

build,

repaint

Finance

:

borrow, loan,

buy, sell.

They can do one such action per turn.

Each agent has a color. Cubes they own are colored their color.

If they build, they deplete the stock pile of raw material cubes and their tower grows by one cube of their color

If the raw material pile goes below 50% then all the agents die.
